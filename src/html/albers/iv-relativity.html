A color has many faces
—the relativity of color

Color is the most relative medium in art

This part of the book has students create small rectangles of paper of the same color, placing them on larger backgrounds of different colors in order to see the differences. What I've chosen to do here is create an interactive tool that will display effectively random colors in this way and you can then say how different each is.
