Ligher and/or darker
—light intensity, lightness

The goal of this is to learn that distinguishing color based on lightness is not so easy. You'll be shown several colors and asked to sort them based on their lightness—how close they are to black or white.

You'll see your accuracy, then be asked if you want to try again with similarly-randomized colors or to repeat with a more difficult challenge. The colors will become harder and harder to distinguish.
